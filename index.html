    About Me

    Welcome!

    I am broadly interested in probability theory and its application to statistical physics, combinatorics, and graph theory. In particular, I mostly spend time in studying the geometry of directed polymers, working on large deviations aspects of models within KPZ universality class, and understanding atypical permutations and its statistics.

    I received my PhD in Mathematics from Columbia University. My advisor was Ivan Corwin

    My list of publications is available in my Research page.
